IACS

The Institute for Applied Computational Science (site) is home to a unique interdisciplinary education and research program in computational science and engineering (CSE) at Harvard’s School of Engineering and Applied Sciences, focused on the use of computation to power discovery and innovation. The Institute's two master’s degree programs train students to solve real-world problems with mathematical models, algorithms, systems innovations, computational techniques, and statistical tools.
